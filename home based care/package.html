Advantages of home births

  • Offers a familiar environment which brings comfort.
  • Brings families,friends together by particiting in child births.
  • Its safer than hospital birth.
  • The giving birth mother will receive maximum attention of those that are present as the focus of all those present is to meet her needs.
  • There is no interference and unnessecary interventions.
  • Risk of infection is minimised for both the mother and the child.
  • There is no interruptions caused by change of shift for the medical team.
  • She does have to worry about when to go to the hospital since her care givers come to her.
  • Continous assessment of the baby during and after birth.
Advantages of home visits
  • Daily administering of drugs and injection for diseases like tuberclosis and diabetes.
  • Reduces the chances of defaulting of taking of drugs like ARVs which is sited as one of the main causes why people who are hiv positive prematurely die.
  • Early dictation of diseases.
  • Some people die of diseases that could have been cured if they were dictated early.
  • Helps to protect confediality.
  • Creates a close patient to nurse relationship.
  • The medical personal is able to attend well to the patient as he or she will no be under pressure.
