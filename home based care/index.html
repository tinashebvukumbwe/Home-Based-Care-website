About our company

Data pond was established to provide convenients and standard health care to special people around the country to their door steps, in the comfort of their homes thus serving extra money and time while maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Data Pond was established after we saw the problems and hardship that people go through to access medical care, getting it late and at times failing to get it all. Time, life and money is wasted along this process due to this and other reasons.

  • No proper medical records.
  • Hospital distance.
  • Bad roads and transport.

Data Pond is here to provide you with convenience and standard health care by

  • Safe and confidential keeping of your medical records and history.
  • Home treatment.
  • Call center with a rapid response.
  • Well equiped with highly experienced and skilled team of health care proffesionals who consistently deliver excellent health care to patients.
